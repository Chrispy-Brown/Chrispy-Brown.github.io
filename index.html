What currency should I use, and are credit cards widely accepted? ▾
The currency in the U.K. is the British Pound (GBP). Credit and debit cards are widely accepted, including at small shops and many taxis. Contactless payment is very common.
Do I need to tip in the U.K., and what is the norm? ▾
Tipping is less customary than in some other countries. Restaurants sometimes add a service charge (around 10–12%); if not, a small tip is appreciated but not expected.
What weather should I expect in England in early August? ▾
Early August is usually mild: roughly 18–24°C (mid-60s to mid-70s °F). Weather can be changeable, so light layers and a compact umbrella are sensible.
